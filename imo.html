Our first kiss
To you, imene the pulse that resides in the left side of my chest and the most beautiful destiny that made me so lucky to love you. I write these words to be your companion during this decisive stage to brush away the dust of hesitation from your heart and plant in its place a certainty that we have an appointment with beauty. I know you are busy now thinking about driving and that license which will open the doors to the privacy we have always dreamed of. But I want you to know that to me, you steered the ship of my heart to safety from the very first moment I met you. You possess a special magic calmness that unsettles my chaos and an intelligence that makes me certain the car will fall in love with you just as I did. Someone who could tame my feelings and make me a prisoner of her smile will find no difficulty in controlling a steering wheel or a road. Be calm, my love. Every "traffic light" you pass is, in reality, bringing you closer to my embrace and to that moment we have long pictured in our imaginations. A moment where we are together under one roof, in that vehicle that will become our small, private kingdom—where no eyes follow us and no noise interrupts our solitude. Just you and I, our shared scent, and the silence that precedes a beautiful storm of emotions. The Promise of the First Moment I know, my soulmate, that the thought of our "first kiss" stirs a wave of shyness and hesitation within you. I know you ask yourself if you are ready. But let me whisper in your ear: this shyness is what makes you the "imane" I loved; it is what grants this moment an indescribable beauty. Do not look at it as an act that worries you, but rather as a quiet melting of all the longings that have accumulated between us through letters and words. We both want it. As much as you are afraid, you long for it, because it will be the "final signature" on the covenant of our love. Imagine that moment: when the car engine stops and everything around us falls silent, and I turn to you so we can each see our reflection in the other's eyes. There will be no need for talk, for our breath will do the work. There will be no hesitation, for our souls will embrace before our lips ever meet. That kiss in the car will be the grand prize—not just because of the achievement, but because we finally secured the moment of solitude that you deserve, and that my heart, weary from waiting, deserves. Building Our Future I want you to be proud of yourself, my love. Every step you take now is a building block for our future. That car you are learning to drive will be the first witness to our deep conversations, our stifled laughs, and those glances we steal in the middle of traffic. Our first kiss there won't just be a memory; it will be the spark that ignites a lifetime of love. Be confident that I will be by your side. I will protect your shyness and cradle your hesitation until it transforms into a burning desire to stay close to me. You are not just a "student" learning to drive; you are a Queen preparing to take her throne in my heart. At every turn in the road, I will be there waiting to tell you: "Well done, Eman. Come to me, for my only place is by your side." Let us make of this appointment a legend we tell ourselves later. Let us make the silence of the car a symphony of love, and that kiss an unbreakable promise that I am yours and you are mine, no matter how long the roads or how different the destinations. I love you, imene. I await your success so I can gift your heart the most precious thing I own: my soul, and a kiss that summarizes the entire meaning of existence.
Our very first kiss, the first in our life together.
